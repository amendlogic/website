## 3. Data We Collect & How We Collect It

### 3.1 Data You Provide Directly

**a) Subscription Registration (via Whop)**
Our subscription checkout is handled entirely by Whop. During checkout on the Whop platform, Whop collects your name, email address, billing address, and payment details. We do **not** operate or control the Whop checkout form and do **not** receive your payment card details at any point.

After a successful subscription, Whop shares the following data with us:
- Email address
- Whop User ID
- Subscription status and plan details
- TradingView username (as entered by you during checkout)

**b) TradingView Username**
Your TradingView username is required to authorize access to the invite-only strategy script. You provide this during the Whop checkout process. We use it solely to grant and manage your TradingView script access.

**c) Contact & Support Inquiries**
When you contact us via email:
- Name (if provided)
- Email address
- Content of your message and any attachments

### 3.2 Data Collected Automatically

**a) Server Logs & Technical Data (via Cloudflare)**
When you visit our Website, Cloudflare automatically records:
- IP address (anonymized or full, depending on configuration)
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Referring URL
- Pages visited and timestamps
- HTTP status codes

**b) Analytics Data (via Google Analytics 4)**
We use Google Analytics 4 ("GA4") to understand how visitors interact with our Website. GA4 collects:
- Pages viewed and navigation paths
- Session duration and bounce rate
- Device type, browser, and operating system
- Approximate geographic location (country/region, derived from anonymized IP)
- Referral source
- Conversion events (e.g., pricing page views, trial sign-ups)

**IP Anonymization:** GA4 is configured to anonymize IP addresses before processing. Full IP addresses are not retained.

Legal basis: **Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR — Consent** (via cookie banner).

### 3.3 Data We Do Not Collect
We do not collect or store:
- Payment card details (handled exclusively by Whop)
- Full billing addresses (collected by Whop, not shared with us in full)
- Sensitive personal data under Art. 9 GDPR
- Trading account credentials, API keys, or exchange login details
- Precise geolocation data
