## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We may also use local storage, session storage, pixel tags, and JavaScript trackers. The term "cookies" in this policy refers collectively to all such technologies.

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

Essential for the proper operation of the website. No consent required.

| Cookie Name | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|----------|---------|----------|
| `_cfuvid` | Cloudflare | Rate limiting and abuse prevention | Session |
| `cf_clearance` | Cloudflare | Records a passed Cloudflare security check | 1 year |
| `cc_cookie` | {SITE.owner.companyName} | Stores your cookie consent decision | 1 year |

**Legal basis:** Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR — Legitimate interests.

### 3.2 Analytics Cookies

Collect anonymised data on website usage. **Only set with your explicit consent** via our cookie banner.

#### Google Analytics 4 (GA4)
Provided by Google Ireland Limited (Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland).

- IP addresses are **anonymised** prior to storage
- Not used for advertising or remarketing purposes
- Data retention limited to **14 months**
- Data minimisation settings enabled

| Cookie Name | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|----------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics | Distinguishes unique users; stores client ID | 2 years |
| `_ga_[ID]` | Google Analytics | Stores and counts page views | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics | User distinction; renewed daily | 24 hours |

**Legal basis:** Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR — Consent.

**Opt-out:**
- Decline analytics cookies in the cookie banner (recommended)
- Install the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

---

### 3.3 Functional Cookies

Set when embedded YouTube videos are actively played. **Only activated with your explicit consent** via our cookie banner.

#### YouTube (Google Ireland Limited)
Provided by Google Ireland Limited (Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland).

- Videos are embedded using **Privacy-Enhanced Mode** (`youtube-nocookie.com`)
- **No cookies are set** before a video is played
- Upon active playback, data including IP address is transmitted to YouTube
- Data transfer to the USA under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)

| Cookie Name | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|----------|---------|----------|
| `VISITOR_INFO1_LIVE` | YouTube | Estimates user bandwidth for video playback | 6 months |
| `YSC` | YouTube | Prevents abusive use; unique video session ID | Session |
| `yt-remote-device-id` | YouTube | Stores user video preferences | Persistent |

**Legal basis:** Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR — Consent.

## 4. Third-Party Cookies

### 4.1 Whop Inc.
Our subscription checkout and member management run entirely on the Whop platform at `whop.com`. When you are redirected to the Whop checkout, you leave our website and enter Whop's environment. Cookies set by Whop on their platform are governed by Whop's own Privacy Policy. We have neither control over nor visibility into these cookies. Please read Whop's Privacy Policy at [https://whop.com/privacy-policy/](https://whop.com/privacy-policy/).

### 4.2 Cloudflare
Cloudflare provides hosting, CDN, and security features and may set necessary security cookies. Details: [https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)

### 4.3 Google Analytics
GA4 sets cookies upon consent. Data is transferred to the USA under Standard Contractual Clauses. Details: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 4.4 YouTube (Google Ireland Limited)
Our website embeds YouTube videos. We use Privacy-Enhanced Mode (`youtube-nocookie.com`) — no cookies are set before a video is actively played. Upon playback, YouTube may set cookies and transmit data to Google Ireland Limited and servers in the USA (basis: Standard Contractual Clauses). Details: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 5. Consent and Your Choices

### 5.1 Cookie Banner
On your first visit, our banner will appear with the following options:
- **Accept all** — necessary, analytics, and functional cookies active
- **Decline** — necessary cookies only
- **Manage settings** — selective choice

Your decision is stored for **1 year**.

- Click **"Cookie Settings"** in the website footer
- Delete browser cookies and revisit the website
